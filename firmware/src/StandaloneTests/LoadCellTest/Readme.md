### CONNECTION DETAILS (ARDUINO MEGA) :
Load Cell | Color Code | J25 JST connection
--- | --- | ---
E+ | Red | 1
E- | Black | 2
A- | White | 3
A+ | Green | 4


Load Cell | Arduino Mega 
--- | --- 
DT | D9 
SCK | D8
